Day : 1 - Nai Roshni

[ Monday,2018 June 25 ]

"Nai Roshni" program for leadership Development of Minority women was inaugurated by Sadbhawna Samiti's President Ms. Aparna Mishra. The program was supported by Ministry of Minority Affairs and the objective is to aware minority women about their rights, life skills, leadership development, digital literacy, health & hygiene, Swachha bharat mission etc. This training will be of 6 days and some stipend will also be paid to trainees
