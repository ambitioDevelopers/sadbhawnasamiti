who we are

Sadbhawna Samiti was founded by Dr.K.N. Mishra with the noble objective of equitable development of disadvantaged and the less fortunate and to usher them into the mainstream society. In order to enhance its contribution in the human development sector, a seprate division named Sadbhawna Centre for Social Research and Development (SCSRD) was founded in 2008 to take up research, impact assessment and monitoring and evalution (M&E)studies as well as large-scale surveys and capacity building assignment.Promoted by eminent expert having decades of experience in the development sector.Over the years, we have caried out more than 150 different assignment for different clients. The organization has exprienced manpower who have worked in both urban as well as rural areas of the Northern and Western states of country including Uttar Pradesh,Madhya Pradesh,Bihar,Jharkhand,Gujarat,Himachal Pradesh,Rajasthan,Uttarakhand and Chhattisgarh. The team members have experience of working at the district,block and village level.
