Who We Are

[ Our History, Our Values and Culture ]

Sadbhawna Samiti was founded by Dr.K.N. Mishra with the noble objective of equitable development of disadvantaged and the less fortunate and to usher them into the mainstream society. In order to enhance its contribution in the human development sector, a seprate division named Sadbhawna Centre for Social Research and Development (SCSRD) was founded in 2008 to take up research, impact assessment and monitoring and evalution (M&E)studies as well as large-scale surveys and capacity building assignment.Promoted by eminent expert having decades of experience in the development sector.Over the years, we have caried out more than 150 different assignment for different clients. The organization has exprienced manpower who have worked in both urban as well as rural areas of the Northern and Western states of country including Uttar Pradesh,Madhya Pradesh,Bihar,Jharkhand,Gujarat,Himachal Pradesh,Rajasthan,Uttarakhand and Chhattisgarh. The team members have experience of working at the district,block and village level.

Having existed since 1997, Sadbhawna Samiti aims at providing equitable development to the underprivileged and disadvantaged people in order to get them into the mainstream society. The mission of the organisation is to:

  • Undertake research, training, and consultancy assignments for various government and non government agencies.
  • To educate men, women and children belonging to the disadvantaged sections of the society living in remote rural areas.
  • To provide economic security to the rural poor through micro insurance and group insurance projects.
  • To boost the agriculture sector by bringing new technologies within the reach of farmers for increased agriculture productivity with sustainable water use.
  • To generate community awareness and mobilize them through IEC activities.

About Our

Founder
Chairperson

Sadbhawna Samiti was founded by late Dr. K.N. Mishra with the noble objective of equitable development. On 25th March, 1998, a school named ‘Din Dayal Mand Buddhi Viklang Vidyalaya’ was opened by Dr. K.N. Mishra, for children with special needs in Barabanki. Sir is also a great poet and has compiled his poems in a book called 'Chayanika’. He has also written a book called ‘Sushila’.

The chairperson of Sadbhawna Samiti, Ms Aparna Mishra has dedicated herself to this organisation which was founded by her father, late Dr. K. N. Mishra on 15th August 1994.She was felicitated with ‘Swayam Siddha Award’ which was given by honourable cabinet minister Shrimati Rita Bahuguna Joshi for outstanding work in entrepreneurship by Anupma Foundation. Apart from the NGO, she owns Sadbhawna hospital, has wellness clinics, is working in the field of organic agriculture, owns a school and also has work related to real estate. She has a big focus on education and health of those who can’t access it especially women. Her hospital has special focus on the reproductive health of women such that there is facility to provide them access to safe and quality treatment. To promote communal harmony she has given charity for the construction of mosques and temples in Barabanki. As a woman, she believes it is important to give girls the opportunity to grow and fulfil their dreams instead of getting them married at an early age. The idea of equality and inclusion, which for her is the basic aspect of human right, has kept her going and there is still a long way for her to go.
