Having existed since 1997, Sadbhawna Samiti aims at providing equitable development to the underprivileged and disadvantaged people in order to get them into the mainstream society. The mission of the organisation is to:

  • Undertake research, training, and consultancy assignments for various government and non government agencies.
  • To educate men, women and children belonging to the disadvantaged sections of the society living in remote rural areas.
  • To provide economic security to the rural poor through micro insurance and group insurance projects.
  • To boost the agriculture sector by bringing new technologies within the reach of farmers for increased agriculture productivity with sustainable water use.
  • To generate community awareness and mobilize them through IEC activities.
