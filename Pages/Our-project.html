कोई दीवाना कहता है

(एक शाम मानवता और कविता के नाम)

Sadbhawna Samiti is organising a charitable event called ‘Koi Deewana Kehta Hai’ (an evening on humanity and poetry) at Sangeet Natya Academy. It is dedicated to the work and life of late Dr. K. N. Mishra (founder of Sadbhwana Samiti) who himself was a poet. This event shall commence with giving gifts (like wheelchair, crutches etc) to the specially abled children and their parents. This will be followed by a Kavi Sammelan which has mesmerizing performances by three eminent poets of the country; Dr. KUMAR VISHWAS, Dr. ANJUM REHBAR, Dr. NIRMAL DARSHAN. It is in our city of Nawabs, where people passionately lend their ears to listen to the language of poetry which unites them all, over different emotions that comes out of words! This is the beginning of a never ending celebration of poetry. We want to create the magical aura of poetry in our city which already beams of art and culture.

Venue: Sant Gadge Ji Maharaj Auditorium, Bypass Road, Near Reserve Bank of India, Vipin Khand, Gomti Nagar, Uttar Pradesh, 226010
For further details, contact : 9839107666, 9838489698

Free Medical Check-up and Medical Care Camp

Sadbhawna Samiti and Sadbhawna Hospital organised a Free Medical Check-up and Medical Care Camp on 30th May, in association with Shine India Society and Grace Cares, a Charitable Foundation of USA. After the Check-up free medicines were also given to the patients. The camp was organised specially for women and children who were actually in need of Medical Care

Water A Flower Day

Recognized each year on May 30 is National Water a Flower Day. This time of year, our flower gardens are in full bloom. Be sure to keep them hydrated. On National Water a Flower Day, this is an opportunity where if we are unable to care for a garden we should try to care for a plant not just today but for forever
